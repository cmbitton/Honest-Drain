Service overview

What hydro jetting actually solves

Cabling cuts a hole through a clog. Hydro jetting cleans the entire inside of the pipe so the line behaves like new. For grease lines, cast iron with scale, and roots in a clay sewer, jetting is the difference between a temporary fix and a real cleaning.

Honest Drain matches nozzle and pressure to the line: a chain or chisel nozzle for scale, a flushing nozzle for grease, and a root-cutting nozzle for clay sewers. After the line is clean, a camera review confirms the job and documents the pipe condition.

Signs you need hydro jetting

The symptoms that point at this service

The right call depends on the symptom. These are the patterns where hydro jetting is the practical fix instead of another round of cabling.

Cabling fixes the clog for two weeks at a timeThe wall of the pipe is fouled; cabling cannot scrub it.
Grease ring visible on the pipe wallJetting is the right cleaning method for greasy lines.
Roots returning every springRoot-cutting nozzles plus jetting reset the line.
