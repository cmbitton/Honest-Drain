Driveway drain cleaning that prevents garage and basement flooding

Service overview

What driveway drain cleaning actually solves

A driveway drain is the lowest point of the driveway and the first thing the water sees during a heavy rain. When leaves, sand, and mud fill the basin or the outlet line, the water has nowhere to go but into the garage or against the foundation.

Honest Drain pulls the grates, vacuums the basins, jets the outlet line, and confirms the drain ties into a working storm or daylight outlet.

Signs you need driveway drain cleaning

The symptoms that point at this service

The right call depends on the symptom. These are the patterns where driveway drain cleaning is the practical fix instead of another round of cabling.

Driveway puddles after light rainBasin or outlet is restricted.
Water reaches the garage doorDrain capacity is overwhelmed; clean or replace.
Sand and grit visible in the basinVacuuming is overdue.
Drain appears to back up during heavy rainOutlet may be blocked or pitched poorly.
