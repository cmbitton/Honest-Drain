Overview

What this category covers

Most clogs start with one slow drain and turn into a full backup days later. Honest Drain pairs the right cleaning method with the cause of the clog so the line stays clear longer than it would after a quick rinse and a plunger.

Routine drain cleaning is the cheapest part of drain ownership. Pair it with a camera inspection when clogs return and customers can stop guessing what is happening behind the wall.

Sub-services

Drain Cleaning options

Each sub-service has its own write-up that explains the work, the signs that point at it, and the honest steps Honest Drain takes during the visit.

Emergency Drain Cleaning

When a drain backs up after hours, an emergency response can stop water damage before it spreads. Honest Drain shows up prepared, explains the cause, and clears the line.

Basement Drain Cleaning

Basement floor drains are the last line of defense before water reaches finished floors. Honest Drain clears them, tests them, and explains how to keep them flowing.

Pipe Descaling

Cast iron drains in older homes choke on rust, mineral scale, and tubercles. Mechanical descaling restores the inside diameter without ripping out the pipe.
