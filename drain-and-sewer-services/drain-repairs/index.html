Service overview

What drain repairs actually solves

Drain repairs are about restoring flow when cleaning cannot. A separated fitting, a sagging horizontal run, a corroded section, or a bad transition between materials will keep clogging no matter how often the line is snaked.

Honest Drain inspects the line, prices the repair before any work begins, and protects the work area while the repair is made. Common fixes include replacing a corroded section of cast iron, resetting a kitchen branch with proper slope, and replacing a failed PVC fitting under a sink or in a wall chase.

Signs you need drain repairs

The symptoms that point at this service

The right call depends on the symptom. These are the patterns where drain repairs is the practical fix instead of another round of cabling.

Cleaning lasts only a few weeksPipe defect is catching debris; repair the pipe.
Visible drips at fittingsReplace the failed fitting; do not just tighten.
Floor staining under a fixtureSlow leak that needs a real fix, not a coating.
