Service overview

What drain & sewer maintenance actually solves

Routine maintenance pays for itself the first time a backup does not happen. Honest Drain builds a simple cadence based on the home's age, the materials in use, the count of past backups, and the way the family actually uses kitchen and laundry water.

A typical residential plan combines an annual or semi-annual cabling of high-use lines with a camera review every two or three years. Cast iron homes get descaling sooner; homes with mature trees get root work on a tighter cycle.
