Service overview

What root intrusion repair actually solves

Roots do not break a healthy sewer. They find a hairline crack, a misaligned joint, or a missing seal and grow inward toward the water and nutrients. Cleaning them out helps for a few months; repairing the entry point is the only permanent fix.

Honest Drain locates the intrusion with a camera and a sonde, then chooses between a spot repair on a single joint, a longer section replacement, or a referral to a trenchless contractor when conditions favor that approach.
