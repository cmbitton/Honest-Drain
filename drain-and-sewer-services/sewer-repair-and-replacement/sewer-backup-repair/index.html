Sewer backup repair that fixes the cause, not just the mess

A sewer backup is the symptom of a damaged or restricted line. Repair the underlying defect and the backup does not return on the next heavy use day.

Service overview

What sewer backup repair actually solves

Sewer backups happen when a line cannot pass the volume the home is sending into it. The cause might be a partial collapse, a belly that holds water, a buildup of grease and scale, or roots that closed the diameter past the point of clearing.

Honest Drain treats the backup itself first to stop damage, then runs a camera to find the underlying defect, and prices a real repair so the next holiday weekend does not bring another backup.
