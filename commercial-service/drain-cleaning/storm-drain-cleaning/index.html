Service overview

What storm drain cleaning actually solves

Storm drains protect parking lots, walkways, and tenant entrances from flooding. They also do nothing if the basin is full of debris and the outlet line is blocked.

Honest Drain vacuums the basins, jets the outlet lines, and confirms the system is ready for the next storm. Pre-rainy-season cleaning is the most cost-effective interval.
