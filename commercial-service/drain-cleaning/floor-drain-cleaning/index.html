Drain Cleaning

Commercial floor drain cleaning that keeps kitchens running

Commercial floor drains take everything: grease, food waste, mop water, and detergent. Cleaning them on a cadence prevents the slow drain that closes the kitchen.

Service overview

What floor drain cleaning actually solves

Floor drains in commercial kitchens see grease that residential drains never deal with, plus mop water, food debris, and detergent. The line behind the drain fouls slowly until the day a slow drain becomes a stop-work problem.

Honest Drain cabling plus periodic jetting cleans the inside of the pipe so the drain works at capacity, not just at minimum flow. After-hours service keeps the cleaning out of the operator's open hours.

Signs you need floor drain cleaning

The symptoms that point at this service

The right call depends on the symptom. These are the patterns where floor drain cleaning is the practical fix instead of another round of cabling.

Floor drain holding water at end of shiftBranch line is restricted; clean before backup.
Mop water pooling around the drainDrain capacity is dropping; schedule jetting.
Sour smell near the drainBuild-up in the line; cleaning resets the smell.
Multiple drains slow at onceBranch or main; investigate beyond the floor drain.
