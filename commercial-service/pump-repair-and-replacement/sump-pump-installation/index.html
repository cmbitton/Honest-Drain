Pump Repair and Replacement

Commercial sump pump installation that actually keeps water out

A sump pump is only as good as its float, its discharge line, and its check valve. Installation done right is a 15-year solution; done wrong, it fails in a year.

Service overview

What sump pump installation actually solves

Commercial sump pump installation looks simple and is not. The pump has to match the inflow rate, the discharge line has to be sized and pitched correctly, the check valve has to be the right kind, and the float and controls have to be set so the pump runs but does not short-cycle.

Honest Drain installs sump pumps that hold up. Each install is tested under load, documented for the property file, and paired with honest guidance on backup systems where the cost of a failure is high.

Signs you need sump pump installation

The symptoms that point at this service

The right call depends on the symptom. These are the patterns where sump pump installation is the practical fix instead of another round of cabling.

Pump runs constantly or short-cyclesFloat or sizing is wrong; rebuild or replace.
Pit floods despite a working pumpDischarge line or check valve is the problem.
Pump older than ten yearsReplace before failure rather than after.
No backup system in placeHigh-stakes spaces deserve a battery backup.
