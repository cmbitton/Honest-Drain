Commercial pump repair and replacement for sumps and ejectors

When a pump fails, water has nowhere to go. Honest Drain repairs and replaces sump and ejector pumps in commercial settings with realistic timelines.

Overview

What this category covers

A failed pump in a commercial building shows up fast: water alarms, wet floors, and a leasing problem on the way. Pump work is straightforward when the right pump is matched to the basin, the discharge line is clear, and the controls are wired to actually trip.

Honest Drain handles inspection, repair, and replacement of sump and ejector pumps, plus the discharge lines and check valves that fail along with them.
